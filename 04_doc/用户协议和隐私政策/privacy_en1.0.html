2. How Gree Uses Cookies

To ensure the normal operation of our website, we may at times put a small piece of data known as a cookie on your computer or mobile device. A cookie is a text file stored by a web server on a computer or mobile device, and the content of a cookie can be retrieved or read only by the server that creates the cookie. The text in a cookie often consists of identifiers, site names, and some numbers and characters. Cookies are unique to the browsers you are using and enable the websites to store such data as your preferences or items in your shopping cart.

Most large websites or Internet service providers can "remember" you when you visit the website (using session cookies) or visit the website again (using permanent cookies) to improve user experience. Cookies can help websites remember your settings and your language, font, and other hobbies that you use when browsing them on your computer or mobile device. You do no need to repeat the settings when you redirect to the website every time. If a website does not use cookies, when you direct to a new webpage of this website, you will be considered a new visitor. For example, when you enter detailed data and direct to another webpage, the new webpage cannot identify you or keep your login status. Our cookies are not used for identifying your personal identity, but helping you visit the website more smoothly and conveniently.

If required, you can control or delete cookies.

You can delete all cookies from the computer or mobile device, or disable cookies on most browsers. If cookies are deleted or disabled, every time when you visit our website, you may need to manually adjust some hobby settings.

Gree will not use cookies for any other purposes than mentioned in this document, or collect any personal data for any other purposes.
